What is ImpactX Bridge?

ImpactX Bridge is a national infrastructure platform built to transform how CSR teams and grassroots NGOs connect, collaborate, and create real-world impact.

Today, high-potential social projects struggle to access the right funding. And CSR teams face a maze of unverified proposals, scattered data, and time-consuming coordination. The result? Slower impact. Missed opportunities. Broken trust.

ImpactX Bridge fixes this with verified workflows, intelligent matching, and transparent processes that simplify how funding moves from boardroom to the field.

This isn’t just another dashboard or grant portal. It’s a full-stack digital infrastructure layer—designed to bring credibility, speed, and visibility to India’s CSR and philanthropy ecosystem.

We’re not building a tool. We’re building the rails that move trust, capital, and outcomes across the impact economy.

If you’re a CSR leader looking for vetted, ready-to-fund projects—or an NGO solving urgent problems on the ground—ImpactX Bridge is the missing link built for both sides.

Corporate CSR teams often struggle to discover and engage credible grassroots NGOs. ImpactX Bridge solves this by offering a tech-enabled platform for:

How It Works

1. Onboard & Verify

NGOs and CSR partners complete a streamlined onboarding process. We verify profiles in 3-5 days, ensuring credibility and compliance from the start.

2. Discover & Match

CSR teams discover vetted NGOs through smart filters. Our upcoming AI-powered tool will provide tailored matchmaking based on cause, region, and impact goals.

3. Fund & Track

Corporates fund projects through transparent, milestone-based disbursals. Both partners track fund utilization and progress in real-time on their dashboards.

4. Report & Scale

NGOs submit structured impact reports on the platform. CSR teams receive audit-friendly data to measure ROI and scale successful partnerships.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our pilot program is open to CSR teams from corporations looking to fund projects in Kerala, and grassroots NGOs operating in Kerala that meet our initial verification criteria. We are particularly interested in organizations focused on Healthcare, Education, Environment, and Livelihoods for this phase.

Initially, for our Kerala pilot, we are focusing on NGOs in Healthcare, Education, Environmental Sustainability, and Livelihood/Skill Development. As we expand, we aim to cover a broader range of social sectors.

For NGOs, onboarding and verification during the pilot phase are free. For CSR teams, we offer a tiered subscription model post-pilot, but pilot participation details will be discussed individually. Our goal is to make the platform accessible and valuable for all stakeholders.

We have a multi-stage verification process that includes checking legal registration (e.g., 12A, 80G, CSR-1), financial transparency, past project performance, and governance structures. We aim to provide a roster of 100% verified, impact-ready NGOs.

Our platform will facilitate structured reporting from NGOs, including milestone updates, fund utilization reports, and impact metrics. For CSR teams, we aim to provide consolidated, audit-friendly ESG reports and real-time project tracking dashboards.
