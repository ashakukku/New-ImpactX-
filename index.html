What is ImpactX Bridge?

ImpactX Bridge helps corporate CSR teams find and fund credible grassroots NGOs — through intelligent matchmaking, transparent workflows, and measurable reporting.

“Where purpose meets precision”

Why Now?

Corporate CSR teams often struggle to discover and engage credible grassroots NGOs. ImpactX Bridge solves this by offering a tech-enabled platform for:

  • Discovery
  • Intelligent matchmaking
  • Streamlined funding
  • Measurable reporting

India’s CSR regime (Section 135) is maturing, with 25,000+ companies under compliance pressure. CSR teams are scrambling to find credible partners, manage documentation, and show measurable impact. Legacy systems (email chains, PDFs, consultants) are fragmented and slow. Grassroots NGOs remain invisible to most funders — lacking tools, visibility, and compliance readiness.

“The space is wide open for a lean, India-first player to define the future of CSR infrastructure.”

A Critical Bottleneck

CSR money is wasted in Excel sheets, rigged against the grassroots, buried in consultants — while good NGOs beg for funds meant for them. We are building the system that fixes all of it.

“The future of CSR won’t wait — it needs clarity now.”
