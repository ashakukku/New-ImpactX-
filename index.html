Frequently Asked Questions

Our pilot program is open to CSR teams from corporations looking to fund projects in Kerala, and grassroots NGOs operating in Kerala that meet our initial verification criteria. We are particularly interested in organizations focused on Healthcare, Education, Environment, and Livelihoods for this phase.

Initially, for our Kerala pilot, we are focusing on NGOs in Healthcare, Education, Environmental Sustainability, and Livelihood/Skill Development. As we expand, we aim to cover a broader range of social sectors.

For NGOs, onboarding and verification during the pilot phase are free. For CSR teams, we offer a tiered subscription model post-pilot, but pilot participation details will be discussed individually. Our goal is to make the platform accessible and valuable for all stakeholders.

We have a multi-stage verification process that includes checking legal registration (e.g., 12A, 80G, CSR-1), financial transparency, past project performance, and governance structures. We aim to provide a roster of 100% verified, impact-ready NGOs.

Our platform will facilitate structured reporting from NGOs, including milestone updates, fund utilization reports, and impact metrics. For CSR teams, we aim to provide consolidated, audit-friendly ESG reports and real-time project tracking dashboards.

We're focused on building robust, scalable infrastructure for the social impact sector in India. Our ambition is to be the most trusted and efficient platform for CSR and NGO collaboration, solving critical bottlenecks. Success for us means enabling greater, measurable good on the ground.

We believe we can significantly improve how social impact is funded and managed in India. By creating transparent, efficient connections between those who want to give and those doing incredible work, we contribute to positive change.
